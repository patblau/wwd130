Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create content about our facility and service. We will provide safety instructions, what to prepare for, what to bring, the equipment we offer, and the river routes that we offer. We will provide available camping sites in the area linked to maps for directions, weather updates, activates and events in the area. We will advise on our business partners and offers of savins we can offer. We will have given information to local business and their links. To increase revenue, visitors will be able to schedule their bookings their visit with us. We will provide them the ability to share their experience via content and pictures. We will set up a questionnaire for feedback which will include a grading scale. We will set up a calendar with community events and places they might like to visit as part of their vacation. We will communicate our rules, mission statement, and moto to provide an understanding of what we offer and what is expected of every visitor.

Audience

Our targeted customers are families and friends who are outdoors enthusiast or embrace the camping community. We will promote our facility as being the ideal adventure within the legal age limit and requirements. We aim to draw single adults wanting a get-way with friends, families looking for a fun vacation, along with groups and organization looking for summer camping activities. We provide discounts with business partners in the area that will help make connections within our community easier and for group bookings to encourage an affordable experience with us.

Branding

"Big Water. Big Mountains. Big Fun."
